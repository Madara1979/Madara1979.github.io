Crafting Magic in Wood

At ONEOFF, we don't just make wood products, we conjure them into existence with a sprinkle of artisanal magic. Our workshop in Mango Green Village, A.S. Fortuna Street, Banilad, Mandaue City, Cebu is where the enchantment happens.

We're not your average woodworkers. We're the masterminds behind the most unique and custom-made lasercut, carved, and engraved wood products. From corporate giveaways to one-of-a-kind gifts, we bring your wildest woodcraft dreams to life.

Each piece is meticulously crafted in our workshop, ensuring that every creation is as unique as the person it's made for. No resellers here, just pure, unadulterated woodcrafting wizardry.

Curious Minds Ask

What makes your products unique?

Our products are not just wood, they're stories waiting to be told. Each piece is a masterpiece of creativity and craftsmanship, unlike anything you've seen before.

Can I request a custom design?

Absolutely! We thrive on turning your wildest woodcraft dreams into reality. Let your imagination run wild, and we'll make it happen.

Do you offer rush orders?

While most of our products are made to order, some intricate pieces require a little extra time to work their magic. Quality takes time, but trust us, it's worth the wait.

Are your products customizable?

Yes, most of our products can be customized to your heart's desire. However, some intricate or labor-intensive pieces are created beforehand and are not customizable.

What is the price range of your products?

Our woodcraft wonders range from $20 to $150, ensuring that there's a magical creation for every budget.
